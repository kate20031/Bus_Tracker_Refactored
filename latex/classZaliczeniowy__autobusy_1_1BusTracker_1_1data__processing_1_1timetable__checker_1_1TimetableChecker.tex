\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Class to filter and calculate time differences between timetable entries and real-\/time data. 

Provides methods to filter timetable times that are within an acceptable range from the real-\/time entry and to calculate the absolute time difference between timetable and real-\/time entries.
